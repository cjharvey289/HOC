About Us

We are House of Circus, we are a Leicester based circus group who range in skills. Join us on a Thursday evening (between 18:15 and 19:45) at the Y Theater in Leicester City Center. Each session can range in skills including typical circus skills like juggling (balls and clubs), diablo and devil stick as well as balance related skills such as AcroYoga and slacklining.
Each of these skills if practiced long enough can have impliments in everyday life. Some skills can help with your hand-eye coordination, others can help improve balance.
These weekly sessions are facilitated by 3 individuals (see below in the meet the team section).
